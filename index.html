What is This Website?

Plant Genomes Online is a free, 24-hour, virtual conference that aims to bring together plant genome researchers across the globe. The conference will be held via Zoom webinar on April 27th-28th (starting 8 am Pacific time) and will be organized across six 4-hour sessions. Each session will have a keynote speaker, time for socializing via Topia, and invited talks from the submitted abstracts. Registration is free! Follow us on Twitter to stay up to date with new information and deadlines: @PlantGenoOnline
