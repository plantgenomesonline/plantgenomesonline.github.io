Plant Genomes Online: April 27, 2022

Plant Genomes Online is a free, 24-hour, virtual conference that aims to bring together plant genome researchers across the globe. The conference will be held via Zoom webinar on April 27th-28th (starting 8 am Pacific time) and will be organized across six 4-hour sessions. Each session will have a keynote speakers, dedicated networking breaks, and invited talks from submitted abstracts. Registration is free! Follow us on Twitter to stay up to date with new information and deadlines: @PlantGenoOnline
